Hva gjør vi?


Vi hovedsakelig restaurer og vasker gravstøtter. Vi vasker og maler inn ny sikrft til gravstøtter som har stått ute i mange år uten noe mye stell.

A fishy concept for fly people established in 2018. We offer real seafood — shrimp, calamari, and fish fillets that are juicy and crispy as splash.

You can have our Orangefish in-between brioche buns or a croissant, on top of our loaded fries, or just by itself with smokin’ hot sauces and other fresh dips.

Our mission is to make people feel fly. It’s splashing good.

Målet vårt er å gi en annen mulighet en nytt kjøp for folk som har eldere gravstøtter

Where is OrangeFish now in the industry?

OrangeFish, a fresh and exciting fast food chain, is making waves in the UK with its delicious seafood offerings. With five locations across the country, we serve high-quality, mouth-watering seafood in a convenient fast food setting.

Whether you're craving crispy fish and chips, succulent shrimp, or a tasty seafood platter, OrangeFish has something for everyone. Join us and dive into a sea of flavors at any of our locations today!

How did OrangeFish come to be?

OrangeFish was founded on July 15, 2018, by Gerard L. Luxs. Inspired by his love for seafood and a vision to offer quick, high-quality meals, Gerard opened the first OrangeFish location.

Today, with five locations across the UK, OrangeFish has become a favorite for seafood lovers seeking fast, delicious meals. Our commitment to fresh ingredients and exceptional service remains at the heart of our success. Welcome to OrangeFish, where the ocean meets fast food.
